# Responsible AI

AgentPulse is an advisory system only.

## Safety commitments

- AI outputs are recommendations, not decisions.
- Human review is required before any operational action.
- The system does not transfer money automatically.
- The system does not block accounts automatically.
- The system does not accuse an agent of fraud.
- The system does not use real customer identities or credentials.
- Provider balances remain separated by provider and outlet.

## Explanation policy

- Use careful language such as "unusual activity detected" or "requires review".
- Avoid definitive claims like "fraud detected" or "criminal activity confirmed".
- Show confidence, evidence, and possible false-positive reasons.
- Highlight uncertainty when data is delayed, missing, or conflicting.

## Data policy

- All demo data is synthetic or simulated.
- No real transaction accounts are used.
- No real customer PIN, OTP, or password is requested.

## Review policy

- Alerts can be assigned, acknowledged, investigated, escalated, and resolved.
- Every change should leave a history entry.
- Manual verification is required when the data quality is poor.
